What is Stroke


A stroke, sometimes called a brain attack, occurs when something blocks blood supply to part of the brain or when a blood vessel in the brain bursts. In either case, parts of the brain become damaged or die. A stroke can cause lasting brain damage, long-term disability, or even death. Learn about the health conditions and lifestyle habits that can increase your risk for stroke.


What our Model do?

Our stroke prediction tool is a web-based application that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to predict the risk of stroke in a patient. The tool collects and analyzes data from the patient's medical records to identify risk factors and develop a personalized risk assessment. It then uses the patient's medical history, demographics, lifestyle, and other factors to generate an individualized risk score. Based on the score, the tool provides personalized recommendations for lifestyle changes and other preventative measures to reduce the patient's risk of stroke. The tool can also be used to monitor a patient's risk over time and help in the early detection of stroke.
